About Fighter Jets

Fighter jets are the cutting-edge aircraft used by air forces around the world for air combat missions. These high-performance machines are designed to dominate the skies with speed, precision, and advanced technology.

"A fighter aircraft is a military aircraft designed primarily for air-to-air combat against other aircraft."

With capabilities like stealth and supermaneuverability, fighter jets represent the pinnacle of aerial combat technology.

Types of Fighter Jets

  • Multirole Fighters
  • Interceptor Fighters
  • Stealth Fighters
  • Air Superiority Fighters

Multirole Fighters

Multirole fighters are versatile aircraft capable of performing a variety of missions, including air-to-air combat, air-to-ground attacks, and reconnaissance.

Stealth Fighters

Stealth fighters are designed with low observable technologies, making them hard to detect by radar and giving them a strategic advantage in air combat.

  1. F-22 Raptor
  2. F-35 Lightning II
  3. Sukhoi Su-57
